Adventure(solo trip) places in & outside India

Are you an adventure-seeker who loves to explore new places and do unusual things? Do you prefer indulging in an adrenaline-pumping activity than lazing along a beach? Then, take our recommendation list of the best adventure places in and outside India into consideration, and get ready with your backpack to venture off the unbeaten tracks for an experiential holiday. Have a sneak-peek at the below listing that has been curated comprising the travel destinations that are meant to inject some adrenaline to your next excursion.

Top 3 International Destinations for Adventurous Souls
Alaska

Decked with glacier-carved fjords and icebergs, the US state of Alaska is one of the best adventurous places to visit outside India. Offering some of the world’s best whitewater rafting and other thrilling experiences, such as heli-skiing, zip lining, glacier hiking, and kayaking through fjords and icebergs, there is no other place in the world that can beat Alaska when it comes to adventure. What’s more? Here, you may also grab a chance to explore wildlife sightings, dip into hot springs, and a lot more.

New Zealand

With some incredibly thrilling experiences that can surely pump your adrenaline like nothing, New Zealand is, undoubtedly, one of the most adventurous places in the world. Also referred to as the land of adventure, it lets you enjoy bungee jumping from the height of 16,500 feet in Able Tasman, whitewater rafting in freezing glacier waters, ice climbing on Fox Glacier, skydiving from 15,000 feet in Queenstown, paragliding through the majestic landscapes, and even swimming with dolphins.

Cape Town

Boasting some of the breathtaking sightseeing options and famed for wine tasting, Cape Town has a lot more to offer to its visitors. Covering a wide spectrum of thrilling experiences, from hiking to sailing through the open seas, and paragliding from Lion’s Head or Signal Hill, an expedition to this majestic location can easily convince you to list it amongst the best adventure places in and outside India. What’s more? Besides the usual adventure sports, here you can also enjoy a rendezvous with the white sharks through shark cage diving activity, which is undoubtedly spellbinding.
